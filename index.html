About Me

Welcome to my poetry haven! I'm Miss Mariba, a passionate poet who finds solace in the beauty of words. Through my poems, I strive to capture the emotions of human life, fleeting moments, and the intricate stories that often go untold. Each piece is a reflection of my thoughts, observations, and dreams, carefully woven to touch hearts and inspire minds. Writing poems has been my way of connecting with the world and sharing the artistry that lies within language. I hope my words resonate with you and bring a little more inspiration and wonder to your day.
